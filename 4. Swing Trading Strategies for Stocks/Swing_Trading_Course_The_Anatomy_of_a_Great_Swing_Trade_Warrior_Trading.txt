In this chapter, I'll teach about makes a stock a candidate for being a great swing trade. Some of the questions to ask yourself are:

Do I understand what the company does?

Does the stock have range?

Does the stock have a defined catalyst?

Can I manage my risk?

Does the company have a competitive advantage?

Are respected investors involved?

What is the fiscal health of the company?

Is there strong leadership?

When is the next earnings announcement?

Is the stock setup for a technical entry?
